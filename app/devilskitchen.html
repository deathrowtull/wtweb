DEVIL'S KITCHEN

2017 Iconic Photograph Re-creation

  • Tuesday, October 24th at 11:30 am
    River Falls Event Center
    Barbecue Lunch followed by Photo Shoot
    Barbecue sponsored by Underwood Law Firm

    Directions
    Go east on the Old Claude Highway (East FM 1151)
    Once at River Falls, you can follow the signs to the event

What are the goals of this project? We are re-creating the iconic photograph a century later and confirming key participants in our area’s history at this time. While accomplishing those goals, we hope to create a meaningful endowment for the enhancement of WTAMU’s Amarillo Center.

How will we get to Devil’s Kitchen? We have transportation arranged for all from the River Falls Event Center to Devil’s Kitchen. For those unable to walk down the final hill to Devil’s Kitchen we have arranged transportation on All Terrain Vehicles. Please let us know if you will need assistance in advance. Facilities will be available at the photo site.

What should I wear? Wear business attire or “Sunday best”. We will be walking in Palo Duro Canyon. Please consider the terrain as you select your foot wear.

What time will we be done? We will begin moving everyone to Devil’s Kitchen between 12:30 and 1 pm. We expect to be done by 3 pm.

Who owns the photograph? WTAMU Foundation will own the rights to the photograph and each participant will need to sign an authorization facilitating future use by the Foundation.

What are the plans for use of the photograph? The plans continue to develop. A print of the picture will be placed with a print of the original in the new Amarillo Center building in downtown Amarillo. Claudia Stuart, co-chair of the Devil’s Kitchen Committee and several faculty members are working on a book to be published about the project and participants. Each participant will receive a copy of the photograph & the book.

What is the weather is poor? Please include an emergency contact number when returning your information. This is how we will get in touch with you in case there is a weather related delay or rescheduling.

Will my family members or friends be able to watch as the photograph is taken? Due to limited space, we will be unable to accommodate guests at the photo site.
